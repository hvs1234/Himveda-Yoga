What We Offer 

  We offer diverse yoga classes focusing on flexibility, strength, mindfulness, relaxation, and holistic well-being, guided by experienced instructors.  

  200-Hour Teacher Training Course (TTC)

Our 200-hour TTC helps you become a certified yoga teacher or deepen your own practice. We combine traditional yoga teachings with modern techniques to give you a complete education.

You’ll learn various styles of yoga, anatomy, physiology, meditation, and teaching methodologies. Our experienced instructors provide personalized guidance to help you grow physically, mentally, and spiritually. Join us to transform your life and inspire others.

  Our Retreats

Our retreats provide a relaxing break from everyday life. Enjoy yoga, meditation, and the beauty of nature. Our retreats are suitable for everyone, from beginners to experienced practitioners.

We have the opportunity to connect with like-minded individuals, nourish your body with healthy meals, and rejuvenate your mind. Escape the daily grind and return home refreshed and reenergized. Experience the tranquility and find your inner peace in our serene retreat settings.

  Healing Sessions

Our healing sessions use various methods to improve your physical, mental, and emotional health. Our experienced healers will help you find balance and well-being. Classified in two ways:-

  Sound Healing

  Pranic Healing
